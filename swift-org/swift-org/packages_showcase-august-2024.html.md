# Community Showcase: August 2024

 
 
 
 

 
 [View this month’s Community Showcase](/packages/showcase.html)
 

 
 The Community Showcase of Swift packages features packages from around the Swift community nominated via
 [this thread on the Swift forums](https://forums.swift.org/t/68168) and voted on by the
 [Swift Website Workgroup](https://swift.org/website-workgroup/).
 
 **This list is an archive of the Community Showcase from August 2024**.

  
- 
 
 
 DiscordBM
 by DiscordBM
 
 A multiplatform Discord library, primarily for making bots. It has full support for Swift concurrency and type-safe APIs with abstractions for easier testability.

 
 
 
 
 Apple
 
 
 
 
 5.10+
 
 
 Apache 2.0
 
 
 
 Nominated in [this forum post](https://forums.swift.org/t/nominations-for-the-packages-community-showcase-on-swift-org/68168/74).
